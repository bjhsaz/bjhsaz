Our Business Models

Tailored solutions for the global mobile application and gaming industry.

1. Project Operation

We provide full-lifecycle operation services for mobile apps and games. Our expert team takes over the management to ensure maximum market reach and revenue performance.

  • Global App Store & Google Play Optimization (ASO)
  • User Acquisition and Retention Management
  • Market-specific Growth Strategies (Europe & America Focus)
  • Continuous Performance Monitoring and Data Analysis

2. Project Development

Leveraging our technical expertise, we provide custom R&D services for clients looking for high-quality mobile solutions.

  • Native iOS and Android Development
  • High-performance Cross-platform Game Engines
  • Custom Backend Architecture & API Integration
  • UI/UX Design Optimized for Global User Habits

We design, develop, and operate our own innovative mobile projects, implementing diverse and effective monetization models.

In-App Subscriptions In-App Purchases (IAP) Premium Features VIP Programs Ad Monetization

Our self-developed projects are built with a focus on long-term user value and sustainable revenue streams across global platforms.
